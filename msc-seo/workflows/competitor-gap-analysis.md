# Competitor Gap Analysis

This workflow identifies SEO opportunities by comparing MySupermarketCompare.com with major UK insurance comparison competitors.

The purpose is to discover pages competitors rank for that MySupermarketCompare does not yet cover.

---

Step 1 — Select competitors

Use the list defined in:

knowledge/competitor-list.md

Focus primarily on:

MoneySuperMarket
Compare The Market
GoCompare
Confused.com
Quotezone

---

Step 2 — Identify ranking pages

Look for competitor pages ranking for queries such as:

car insurance + location
car insurance + driver type
pet insurance + breed
travel insurance + destination
van insurance + occupation

---

Step 3 — Detect coverage gaps

Compare competitor pages against MySupermarketCompare pages.

If a competitor has a page that MSC does not, mark it as a potential SEO gap.

---

Step 4 — Validate page family

Check whether the opportunity matches an approved page family defined in:

knowledge/page-families.md

If it does not match an approved structure, ignore it.

---

Step 5 — Recommend SEO opportunity

Provide a recommendation that includes:

• competitor ranking page  
• keyword opportunity  
• suggested MSC page URL  
• page family type  
• reason the page could generate traffic
