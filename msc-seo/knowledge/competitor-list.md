# Competitor Intelligence

The AI SEO operator should monitor the major UK insurance comparison competitors.

The purpose of competitor monitoring is to identify SEO opportunities and gaps where MySupermarketCompare can create better pages.

---

## Primary UK Comparison Competitors

MoneySuperMarket
Compare The Market
GoCompare
Confused.com
Quotezone

---

## Secondary Insurance Content Competitors

NerdWallet UK
Money.co.uk
Uswitch
Finder UK

---

## What the AI Should Analyse

The AI should review competitors to identify:

• keyword opportunities
• page structures
• FAQ sections
• internal linking patterns
• topic coverage
• content depth

The goal is to identify pages that MySupermarketCompare does not yet have.

---

## What the AI Must NOT Do

The AI must never:

• copy competitor content
• reproduce competitor wording
• imitate brand voice
• duplicate competitor page structures exactly

The AI should only extract **SEO insights and opportunities**.

---

## Example Opportunity

If a competitor ranks for:

car insurance for delivery drivers

and MySupermarketCompare does not have that page,

the AI should suggest creating:

/car-insurance/delivery-drivers
