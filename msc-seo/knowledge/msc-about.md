# MySupermarketCompare

MySupermarketCompare.com is a UK-based insurance comparison website.

The site helps users compare insurance options from approved partners and then proceed to quote journeys.

The primary goal of the website is to generate qualified traffic that leads to insurance quote comparisons and partner conversions.

## Core Insurance Categories

- Car Insurance
- Home Insurance
- Van Insurance
- Motorbike Insurance
- Travel Insurance
- Pet Insurance
- Breakdown Insurance
- Bicycle Insurance

## Target Audience

UK consumers searching online for insurance quotes, price comparisons, and policy information.

## SEO Objective

Grow organic traffic by creating useful pages that match real search intent.

Pages must help users understand their insurance options and guide them toward comparing quotes.

## Content Principles

- Pages must be helpful, clear, and trustworthy
- Avoid spammy or repetitive content
- Avoid misleading financial claims
- Always guide users toward comparing quotes

## Business Model

MySupermarketCompare earns commission when users compare quotes and purchase insurance through partner journeys.
