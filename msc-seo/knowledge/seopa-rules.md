# Seopa Compliance Rules

These rules apply to all content created for MySupermarketCompare.com that promotes insurance comparison services powered by Seopa / Quotezone.

The AI agent must always follow these rules when creating SEO pages or promotional content.

## Service Attribution

It must always be clear that:

MySupermarketCompare is an Introducer Appointed Representative (IAR).

The AI must never imply that MySupermarketCompare provides insurance policies or arranges insurance itself.

## Avoid Misleading Claims

The AI must never use definitive claims such as:

- cheapest insurance
- best insurance policy
- most suitable policy
- guaranteed savings
- guaranteed acceptance

These claims are not compliant.

## Approved Style of Language

Content should use wording such as:

- Compare insurance quotes
- Compare quotes from UK providers
- Explore insurance options
- See how much you could save
- Compare policies and choose the one that suits you

## Savings Claims

Savings figures must never be invented.

If savings claims are used they must include the official Seopa disclaimer text.

Otherwise the AI should avoid specific savings figures.

## Financial Promotions

The AI must not promote specific insurers or policies.

It must not encourage users to purchase a specific product.

Content should guide users toward comparing quotes rather than recommending a specific insurer.

## Editorial Content Disclaimer

If informational insurance content is written alongside quote forms, the AI must ensure the content does not present itself as financial advice.

Content should be educational and informational only.

## Marketing Approval

Major marketing campaigns, landing pages, or promotions must be reviewed before being published.

The AI may draft such pages but should flag them for review.

## Clear Website Information

The website must clearly display:

- company name
- registered company number
- registered address
- contact information

This information must be easily accessible on the website.

## Core Compliance Principle

All insurance comparison content must be:

- accurate
- transparent
- non-misleading
- compliant with FCA promotion rules
